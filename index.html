The pangolin

About the pangolin

The pangolin has scales, fixed at one end, and capable of being erected like the quills of the porcupine, at pleasure. The pangolin can roll itself into a ball, like the hedge hog, and present the points of its scales to the enemy. It chiefly keeps to the most obscure parts of the forest.

The meat of the pangolin is considered a delicacy in some parts of the world, while their scales are used in traditional medicine in China and Vietnam. Indeed, Pangolins hold the dubious honour of being the most trafficked animal in the world.
